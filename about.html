About College

Sri Mahaveera College, managed by the Academy of General Education, Manipal, and Sri Mahaveera College Trust, Moodbidri, is a hub for academic and extracurricular excellence. It offers quality education at an affordable cost and has earned an 'A' grade in the 4th NAAC cycle. The college organizes various programs, including the Science and IT Fest, to foster interest in science, technology, and innovation among students.

About NCC

Our College Is First to Start NCC unit in 18 KAR BN in 1986. Our Principal Major, Dr.Radhakrishna was the first ANO who's work was appreciated by the Precident of India So he was honoured with Major rank in 2023. Currently our unit is lead by Lt.Vijayalakshmi Marla. More than 2000 CDTs have stepped their milestone through our Unit and many of them are currently serving under the Arm Force.

About RAKSHAK

NCC Fests provide a platform for cadets to showcase their skills and talents in various areas, such as drill, Obstacles, Weapon Test and Knowledge About Nation. It encourage teamwork, camaraderie, and bonding among cadets from different institutions and backgrounds. It provide opportunities for cadets to develop leadership and organizational skills by participating in planning, execution, and management of events.
