About College

Sri Mahaveera College is managed by The Academy of General Education, Manipal and Sri Mahaveera College Trust, Moodbidri. Mahaveera College will be a center of learning and development by not only organising classroom teaching but also co-curricular and extra-curricular activities. Sri Mahaveera College has been addressing the needs of the society by helping individuals fulfill their dreams of quality education at a very affordable cost. Even in the 4th cycle of NAAC accreditation, our college has been awarded an 'A' Grade.

About NCC

Our college was first to start NCC unit in 18 KAR BN during 1968. Our NCC unit received State Award for the Best Performance in NCC for the year 1988 by Ramakrishna Hegde, Chief Minister of Karnataka. Our college also recevied State Level award for outstanding performance during the year 1988-89. Our Principal Major Dr. Radhakrishna served as Associate NCC Officer for more than 24 years. He received Honorary Major Rank for his committed service and achievements in NCC during 2023. Currently our unit is lead by Lt. Vijayalakshmi Marla. More than 2000 cadets have stepped their milestone through our unit and many of them are currently serving under the Arm Force.

About RAKSHAK

RAKSHAK' - NCC Fest provides a platform for cadets to showcase their skills and talents in various areas such as drill, obstacle training test, weapon test and knowledge about Nation. It encourages teamwork, camaraderie and bonding among cadets from different institutions and backgrounds. This fest provides opportunities for cadets to develop leadership and organizational skills by participating in planning, execution and management of events
